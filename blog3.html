Chasing Goals On and Off the Field

Bilang isang college student, hindi madali ang pagsabayin ang academics at football training. May mga araw na tinatanong ko ang sarili ko kung kakayanin ko ba ang mga deadlines sa school habang nagbibigay din ng oras at effort sa training. This blog shares my personal experience as a student-athlete and how I manage both education and my passion for football.

The Balancing Act of School and Football

Life as a Student and playing football

Being a college student while actively training in football requires discipline and sacrifice. Pagkatapos ng training, madalas pagod na ang katawan ko pero kailangan ko pa ring mag-aral, gumawa ng projects, at mag-review para sa exams. Through this experience, natutunan kong ayusin ang oras ko at maging responsable sa aking priorities.

The Skills That Will Shape My Football Journey

Football taught me important life skills such as discipline, teamwork, consistency, and mental toughness. These skills are not only useful on the field but also inside the classroom. Natutunan kong mag-set ng goals at magpatuloy kahit nahihirapan, which helps me grow as both a student and an athlete.

What Coaches Look for in Student Football Players

Based on my observation, coaches value attitude and commitment as much as talent. Mahalaga ang respeto, discipline, at willingness to learn. As a student-athlete, I aim to show that I can balance academics and sports, proving that responsibility and passion can go together.

My Dream Football Project

My dream is to be part of a football program that supports both education and athletic development. I want to help create opportunities for students who want to pursue football without sacrificing their studies. Through this, I hope to inspire others to chase their passion while staying committed to their education.

Reflections on the Journey

Managing school and football training at the same time is challenging, but it has taught me valuable lessons about discipline and perseverance. My biggest realization is that I do not have to choose between education and football. With proper time management and dedication, I can work hard for both and continue building my future as a student-athlete.
